All savegames here:
  • are completed and legit (no cheats were used unless it's specified)
  • come from PAL versions if possible (though they can usually be used in any region)

I consider a completionist myself and I like to keep backups of my savegames, it's like a trophy for all wasted played hours. And I thought it wouldn't harm anyone if I shared them, hoping they can be useful for someone! :-)
